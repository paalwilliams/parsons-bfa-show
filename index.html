Unbound is an immersive online XR show and website which will exhibit the thesis projects of the Parsons School of Design BFA Photography Class of 2021. Unbound is a community driven initiative conceptualized, curated, and designed by a group of students within the class. In the weeks surrounding the launch of the show there will be scheduled live-streamed programming including panel discussions with visiting artists, artist spotlights, and the release of a student-produced documentary.
